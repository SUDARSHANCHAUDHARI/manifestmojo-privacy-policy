# ManifestMojo Privacy Policy

This repository hosts the official privacy policy for the ManifestMojo Android application.

## Purpose

This repository is intended for:
- Public access to the privacy policy
- Google Play Store linking
- Transparency about data collection and usage

## Privacy Policy

The complete privacy policy can be found in [PRIVACY_POLICY.md](./PRIVACY_POLICY.md).

## Quick Summary

**Key Points:**
- ✅ All data is stored locally on your device
- ✅ No cloud storage or remote servers
- ✅ No user accounts required
- ✅ Journal entries and affirmations never leave your device
- ✅ Only category selection is sent to Google Gemini API for content generation
- ✅ You can export or delete your data at any time
